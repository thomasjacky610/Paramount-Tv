How to Watch Paramount+ on Your TV

Follow this simple guide to stream Paramount+ on your Smart TV, Roku, Firestick, or other devices.

Step 1 — Check Device Compatibility

Make sure your TV or streaming device supports the Paramount+ app (Smart TV, Roku, Firestick, Apple TV, Android TV, etc.).

Step 2 — Open the App Store on Your Device

Go to the app store or channel store on your TV or streaming device and search for the Paramount+ app.

Step 3 — Install the Paramount+ App

Select the app and install it on your device. Wait for the installation to complete.

Step 4 — Open the App

Launch the Paramount+ app from your apps list after installation.

Step 5 — Sign In or Create an Account

Use your existing Paramount+ credentials to sign in, or create a new account if you are a new user.

Step 6 — Activate on Your TV

You may see an activation code on your TV screen. Follow the instructions provided in the app to complete activation.

Tip: Ensure your device is connected to a stable Wi-Fi network for smooth streaming.

Step 7 — Start Streaming

Once activated, you can browse shows and start watching your favorite content on Paramount+.
